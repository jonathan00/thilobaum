DEVELOPED IN
EAST GERMANY,
BUILT FOR ETERNITY

Connoisseurs call him the golden ear. Joachim Kiesler started developing in 1974 what speaker elitists now trade as nothing less than an acoustic revelation. With over half a century of experience the Saxony based company ME-Geithain still develops and constructs all of his creations modularly on-site – leading to independence from third parties, ensuringthat all components deliver unrivaled quality and will be available long-term. This uncompromising dedication to sustainable engineering sets international standards and makes every ME speaker a true investment in the future.

THE K1 LIMITED EDITION INCORPORATES A 3-WAY-COAXIAL ME-SPEAKER TO ACHIEVE THE HIGHEST DEGREE OF SOUND PRECISION POSSIBLE.

Unparalleled acoustic comprehension and in-depth insights on physics research are enabling ME-Geithain to utilize its industry leading inhouse developed coaxial innovation called MCDS Coax. This worldwide unprecedented technology achieves unparalleled homogenous and colouration neutral sound patterns as well as excellent spatiality and unrivaled sound staging.
